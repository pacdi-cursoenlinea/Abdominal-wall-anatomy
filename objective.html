Objective

What will you learn?

In this activity you will learn the parts that constitute abdominal wall, and you will be able to recognize the importance of knowing their anatomy at the moment of performing surgical procedures. You will be able to reach these goals by putting into practice your knowledge through diverse activities that will allow you to identify your strengths and weaknesses when talking about the anatomy of the abdominal wall.
