Abdominal Wall Anatomy

The abdominal wall

The abdominal wall is defined cranially by the xiphoid process of the sternum and the costal margins, and caudally by the iliac bones of the pelvis. It extends to the lumbar spine, which joins the thorax and pelvis and is a point of attachment for some abdominal wall structures

Did you know...?

Did you know that knowledge of the layered structure of the abdominal wall permits efficient and safe entry into the peritoneal cavity?
